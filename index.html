Our Story

Yinare Feng Shui Design Firm was borne out of a labor of love for art, design, and the principal designer's cultural heritage. A member of the International Feng Shui Guild, Yinare studied and completed an apprenticeship under Prof. Yu Xixian at Peking University in Beijing, China before setting up her own practice in Los Angeles. She also leverages her degree in Spatial Experience Design from the ArtCenter College of Design in California to meld Chinese tradition and American design practices, creating her own distinct style of elevated Californian living with a nod to cultural sensitivities.
